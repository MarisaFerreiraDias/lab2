Poema

Atlântida Perdida
Edgar Allan Poe
Out of the blue it came,
A tale from the deep,
Of a city so grand, in the ocean asleep.

Legends speak of its grace,
Lost beneath the waves,
Where time has no place,
And silence enslaves.

Beneath the tranquil sea,
The echoes of its name,
Whisper the mystery,
Of Atlantis' flame.
